Test Case PERF-0020
Title The garage door controller takes less than 1 minute to power up
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • Upon power loss, the controller shall take no longer than 1 minute to recover (able to accept a bluetooth remote commands) once power is again available.

This test case is intended to demonstrate that in situations where the Raspberry PI controller has to reboot (e.g. loss of power), it takes less than a minute to get to a state where it can accept a new bluetooth open/close command.
Setup
  • Controller is initially plugged in.
Actions
  1. Confirm that the controller is able to open and close the garage door by pressing the open/close button on the Android app.
  2. Pull the power connector from the Raspberry PI.
  3. Wait 10 seconds and restore power.
  4. Wait 60 seconds and then command the door state to change from the app.
  5. Verify the door opens or closes.
  6. Repeat step 2 to 5 10 times.
Expected After a reboot, the Android app can send a door command that is successfully processed within 60 seconds.
Results FAILED
Cleanup
Requirements GDO-PERF-020
